SASKIA DE BOK | BSC ARCH

ARCHITECTURAL MANIFESTO

An architectural intern, having recently graduated with a degree BSc Architecture obtained from the University of Pretoria. Immensely passionate about architecture as a means of engaging with the complexities of humanity and its position within the larger social paradigm – specifically in the richly diverse context of South Africa. I am intrigued by the concept of architecture and design that “works hard” (26’10 Architects) - Architecture that not only cultivates activity, but becomes and integral participant in the day to day rituals of the South African user; establishing an oscillating relationship with the individuals whom activate the space.
